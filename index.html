Step 2 of 3

How to capture

Read this carefully — the protocol matters for reconstruction quality.

SINGLE CONTINUOUS VIDEO · ONE TOUR
1
Hold the phone in portrait
Vertical orientation, at about chest height. Don't tilt it during the walk.
2
Camera at the wall on your left
About 1.5–2m from that wall. Walk slowly along the perimeter.
3
Don't stop. Don't pan. Don't rotate in place.
Continuous motion is required. Curve smoothly through corners — let your body rotate naturally.
4
After the perimeter, curve inward
The app will prompt you. Walk in toward the room interior — the table, U-shape, or whatever furniture is at the center — with camera now pointed at it.
5
Walk a clockwise interior loop
Around the furniture cluster at ~1.5m. For non-rectangular shapes (U-shaped tables, multiple tables), trace the outer edge of the furniture as a loop. The app tells you when capture is complete.
